Vada-Pav

Description :-

The "Indian Burger": A simple yet incredibly satisfying vegetarian snack deeply ingrained in Mumbai's culinary identity.

Ingeridents:-

  1. Pav: Fluffy, white dinner roll-like bread.
  2. Batata Vada
  3. Chutneys
  4. Fried Green Chili

How to made:-

Make filling: Boil potatoes, mash, then cook with spices, ginger, garlic, and chilies.

Shape: Form potato mixture into balls.

Batter: Mix chickpea flour with spices and water to make a batter.

Fry: Dip potato balls in batter, then deep-fry until golden.

Assemble: Slit open pav buns, spread with chutneys, insert a hot vada into each.

Enjoy: Serve with extra chutneys and a fried green chili on the side.
