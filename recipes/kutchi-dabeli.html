Kutchi Dabeli - A Flavorful Gujarati Street Food   

Kutchi Dabeli

     

Ingredients

    
        
  • Pav: Soft, small bread rolls similar to hamburger buns.
  • Boiled Potatoes: The base for the filling.
  • Butter: Often used to toast the pav.
    •     
  • Dabeli Masala: A special spice blend that's the heart of the dabeli's flavor. This usually includes:
    • Dry red chilies
    • Coriander seeds
    • Cumin seeds
    • Cloves
    • Cinnamon
    • Black pepper
    • Dry coconut (sometimes)
    • Other spices like star anise, bay leaf, etc. (depending on the recipe)
    •     
  • Tamarind Date Chutney: A sweet and sour chutney.
    •     
  • Garlic Chutney: A spicy, red chili-garlic chutney.
    •     
  • Pomegranate Seeds: Adds sweetness and a burst of fresh texture.
    •     
  • Roasted Peanuts: For crunch.
    •     
  • Sev: Thin, crispy fried noodles made of chickpea flour.
    •     
  • Chopped Onions
    •     
  • Chopped Cilantro (Coriander Leaves)
    •   
  

How It's Made

    
        
  1. Masala and Filling: The dabeli masala is roasted and ground. Potatoes are boiled, mashed, and mixed with the masala to create the filling.
  2. Assembly:
    • Lightly butter the pav and toast until golden.
    • Slit the pav open and slather with the chutneys.
    • Generously stuff the potato filling into the pav.
    3.     
  3. Grilling (optional): The filled pav may be lightly grilled until slightly crisp.
    4.     
  4. Garnishing: Scatter toppings like pomegranate seeds, peanuts, sev, onions, and cilantro over the dabeli just before serving.
